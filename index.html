The Motivation Behind

This service was primarily created for the ease of commuting using jeepney as the mode of transportation. Some places in the Philippines do not have abundant transportation means. Jeepneys are mostly the means of travel all over the Philippines. Although it is part of the Philippine culture, their presence in a region does not suffice to the demand of the population. This project aims to offer a service wherein commuters will have the access to real-time updates about the jeepney status inside the terminal. Aside from that, commuters can book their commute and pay through online transactions. In times where there is not enough number of jeepneys because it is already rush hours, commuters are able to see the number of jeepneys going back to the terminal to pick them up depending to each commuters drop off point.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I Create an Account?

Click Here ! To start creating your account then provide the required credentials to have the full access to our services.

Where is these services Available?

These service is currently limited to the people residing in the southern part of the Legazpi City. It will be a point to point drop off and pick up means.

How do I book my commute?

When everything is set, Log In to the website to start. Then choose your Jeepney's and input the destination. Lastly, choose your payment option and tap CONFIRM.

Can I use JIB with a smartphone?

Certainly, this is web based services. Rest assured it will be compatible to your smartphones and computers. The developers is putting all of their efforts to develop an application for smartphones.
